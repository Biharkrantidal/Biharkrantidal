About Bihar Kranti Dal

Bihar Kranti Dal is a people-focused political movement dedicated to building a strong, educated, developed and corruption-free Bihar. Our aim is to empower youth, support farmers, improve education, create employment opportunities and bring transparent governance for every citizen.

Our Main Agenda

Youth Employment

Creating job opportunities and skill development programs for Bihar's youth.

Education Reform

Improving schools, colleges and digital education facilities across Bihar.

Farmer Support

Fair price, irrigation support and modern farming facilities for farmers.

Women Safety

Ensuring safety, education and empowerment for women in Bihar.

Anti-Corruption

Transparent governance and strict action against corruption.

Development

Better roads, healthcare, electricity, internet and infrastructure.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make Bihar a developed, self-reliant and respected state where every young person gets opportunities, every farmer gets dignity, every student gets quality education and every citizen gets justice.

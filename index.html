About Bihar Kranti Dal

Bihar Kranti Dal is a movement against corruption, paper leaks, unemployment, injustice and the failure of the system. We stand for youth empowerment, women's safety, transparent governance, quality education, job creation, and a strong, self-reliant Bihar.

Our Main Agenda

Youth Employment

Jobs, skill development and real opportunities for Bihar's youth.

Stop Paper Leak

Strict action against exam corruption and protection of students' future.

Women Safety

Safety, dignity and empowerment for every daughter and woman of Bihar.

Anti-Corruption

Transparent governance and accountability from every public system.

Education Reform

Better schools, colleges, teachers and digital education facilities.

Development

Better roads, hospitals, electricity, internet and infrastructure.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make Bihar a developed, fearless, self-reliant and respected state where youth get opportunities, women feel safe, farmers get dignity, students get justice and every citizen lives with pride.

Message from the Founder

My dear youth of Bihar, enough is enough. For too long, corruption, paper leaks, unemployment and a broken system have crushed the dreams of hardworking students and honest families.

Bihar Kranti Dal was born to challenge this failed system with courage, discipline and unity. This movement belongs to every student, every unemployed youth, every farmer, every woman and every citizen who refuses to stay silent.

We do not believe in fear. We believe in action. We do not accept injustice. We demand accountability. We are inspired by the courage and sacrifice of Shaheed Bhagat Singh, Shaheed Chandrashekhar Azad and Shaheed Bharat Bhushan Tiwari.

The time has come for Bihar's youth to rise, organize and build a new Bihar with education, employment, safety, justice and self-respect.
